Common Problems We Solve

Every Service Business Hits the Same Walls

The Founder Bottleneck

Every decision runs through the owner. Staff ask questions that should be answered by documented systems. The business cannot operate without you present — which means you cannot grow, take time off, or focus on high-value work.

Inconsistent Client Experience

The client experience varies depending on which team member handles it. No standardized intake, no documented onboarding process, no consistent communication — so results and impressions vary unpredictably.

Onboarding Takes Too Long

New hires require weeks of personal supervision because nothing is written down. Every new team member has to learn by watching, asking, and making mistakes — instead of following a documented system.

No Systems to Scale

Adding clients, staff, or locations doesn't scale the revenue — it scales the chaos. Without documented operational infrastructure, growth creates more problems than it solves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Business Operations Consulting FAQ

Do you work with non-healthcare businesses?

Yes. While healthcare is our primary focus, the operational systems we build — SOPs, workflow design, intake infrastructure, CRM systems, onboarding frameworks — translate directly to any service-based business. If you have operational gaps limiting your growth, we can help.

We are a startup with no systems at all. Can you help?

Absolutely. Pre-launch or early-stage is the ideal time to build operational infrastructure. We can deliver a complete SOP library, intake system, and onboarding framework before your first hire or first client. Starting with systems in place changes everything.

How is working with SheVada Solutions different from hiring a business coach?

Business coaches advise. We build. Every engagement produces documented, transferable systems your team can use immediately — not recommendations you have to figure out how to implement. We deliver SOPs, workflows, and infrastructure, not frameworks and strategies.

How long does a business operations engagement take?

A focused SOP development project typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full operational infrastructure build takes 4–8 weeks. An ongoing Operations Partner retainer starts at $1,497/month with a 3-month minimum. We establish a clear timeline during the assessment phase.

Common Problems We Solve

Why Home Care Agencies Hit a Wall

⚠
Lost Referrals

Referrals arrive by phone, fax, and email with no centralized tracking system. Leads fall through the cracks, and there is no visibility into how many are being lost or why.

⚠
No Standardized Intake

Every intake call goes differently depending on who handles it. Inconsistent information collection leads to incomplete client records and a frustrating first impression for families in crisis.

⚠
Owner as Bottleneck

Nothing moves without the owner. Scheduling, client decisions, staff questions — all escalate upward because there are no documented systems for the team to follow independently.

⚠
Compliance Exposure

Documentation practices that are inconsistent across caregivers and coordinators create risk during state inspections and accreditation reviews without a standardized documentation protocol.

Frequently Asked Questions

Home Care Agency Consulting FAQ

Do you work with non-medical home care agencies?

Yes. The majority of the operational systems we build — intake, scheduling, coordination, documentation, onboarding — apply directly to non-medical companion care agencies. We understand both the operational and compliance environments specific to home care.

Can you help us build systems before we launch?

Absolutely. Pre-launch is the ideal time to build operational infrastructure. We can deliver a complete SOP library, intake system, and staff onboarding framework before your first client or hire. Starting with systems in place changes everything.

We already have some SOPs. Can you improve them?

Yes. We will assess your existing documentation, identify gaps, and either improve what exists or rebuild it with a consistent structure and format your team can actually follow.

How long does a home care operations engagement take?

A focused intake and SOP project typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full operational infrastructure build takes 4–6 weeks. We establish a clear timeline during the assessment phase and hold to it.

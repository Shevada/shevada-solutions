Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work primarily with healthcare organizations — medical practices, clinics, home care agencies, assisted living facilities, behavioral health practices, med spas, and healthcare startups. We also work with service-based businesses that need operational discipline.

Do you work outside of healthcare?

Yes. While healthcare is our primary focus, the operational systems we build — SOPs, workflow design, intake infrastructure, CRM systems — translate directly to any service-based business with operational gaps limiting growth.

Can you build SOPs from scratch?

Yes. SOP development is one of our core services. We work with you to understand your workflows, document them accurately, and produce written procedures your team can immediately put into use. No existing documentation is required.

What happens during an operational systems audit?

We conduct a structured assessment of your operational environment — reviewing workflows, documentation, communication systems, staff roles, and compliance posture. We identify gaps and deliver a clear implementation roadmap.

Do you provide ongoing operational support?

Yes. The Operations Partner retainer provides ongoing fractional operations support starting at $1,497/month with a minimum 3-month engagement. Custom scopes are available for larger organizations.

How long do projects typically take?

A focused SOP development project typically takes 2–4 weeks. A full operational infrastructure build may take 4–8 weeks. We establish a clear timeline during the assessment phase and hold to it.

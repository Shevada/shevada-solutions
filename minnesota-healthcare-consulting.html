Frequently Asked Questions

Minnesota Healthcare Consulting FAQ

Are you based in Minnesota?

Yes. SheVada Solutions was founded in the Twin Cities by Shevada Tools, a Registered Nurse with 10+ years of direct Minnesota healthcare experience. We serve Minnesota organizations in person and remote organizations nationwide.

Do you understand Minnesota DHS regulations for home care and assisted living?

Yes. With direct experience in Minnesota home care and long-term care environments, we build operational systems and documentation that align with Minnesota Department of Human Services requirements for licensed home care agencies and assisted living facilities.

Can you serve organizations outside the Twin Cities?

Absolutely. We serve healthcare organizations throughout greater Minnesota — Rochester, Duluth, St. Cloud, Mankato, and rural communities statewide — through fully remote engagements that produce the same results as in-person work.

Do you work with Minnesota FQHCs or rural health clinics?

Yes. Federally Qualified Health Centers and rural health clinics in Minnesota face specific operational challenges around documentation, compliance, and care coordination that we have direct experience addressing. We would be glad to discuss your organization's specific needs.

What makes SheVada Solutions different from other Minnesota healthcare consultants?

Active RN licensure. That means the clinical insight embedded in every system we build — the documentation standards, escalation protocols, workflow design — comes from direct nursing experience in Minnesota healthcare environments. Not a business framework applied to healthcare from the outside.
